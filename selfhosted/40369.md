Overview

FreshRSS is a free, open-source, self-hosted RSS feed aggregator that allows users to collect and read updates from multiple websites, blogs, YouTube channels, and online publications in one unified dashboard.

Instead of visiting individual websites, FreshRSS automatically fetches new content via RSS or Atom feeds and displays them in a clean, ad-free, chronological interface. It works entirely on your own server ‚Äî giving you full control, privacy, and offline access to your subscribed content.

Key Features

1 Centralized Reading ‚Äì View all news, articles, and video updates in one dashboard.

2 Automatic Feed Updates ‚Äì Fetches new content periodically via cron or background jobs.

3 Multi-User Support ‚Äì Multiple accounts can be created on the same instance.

4 API Support ‚Äì Compatible with third-party apps such as Reeder, FeedMe, and NetNewsWire.

5 Extensible & Customizable ‚Äì Supports themes, extensions, and custom CSS.

6 Offline & Ad-Free ‚Äì Stores content locally, free from ads or tracking.

7 Lightweight & Fast ‚Äì Works even on low-end hardware (like Raspberry Pi).
